Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI image generation work?

Petadio uses advanced AI technology to analyze your pet photos and generate artistic variations. Simply upload a photo of your pet, and our AI will create unique, stylized images based on your input.

What types of images can I upload?

You can upload clear photos of your pets in good lighting. The AI works best with high-quality images that clearly show your pet's features. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and HEIC.

How long does image generation take?

Generation time typically ranges from 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on the complexity of your request and current server load.

Can I use generated images commercially?

Generated images are intended for personal use. For commercial use, please contact our support team for licensing information.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app settings. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Try restarting the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you have the latest app version
  • Clear the app cache if issues persist
